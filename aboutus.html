公司简介
Company Overview

武汉市金鼎鹤服装材料有限公司（现更名为武汉市金杉鹤商贸有限公司）成立于1998年，办公地址设于风景优美的湖北武汉硚口区唐家巷硚口织布厂内。 公司最初占地3000平方米，集生产和办公为一体，专注于高品质服装材料的研发与制造。

Wuhan Jindinghe Garment Materials Co., Ltd. (now renamed Wuhan Jinshanhe Trading Co., Ltd.) was established in 1998, with its office located in the picturesque Tangjia Lane Qiaokou Weaving Factory, Qiaokou District, Wuhan, Hubei. Initially covering an area of 3,000 square meters, the company integrated production and office spaces, focusing on the research and development and manufacturing of high-quality garment materials.
发展历程
Development History

经过十年的稳健发展，公司在湖北孝子董永的故乡——孝感市庙头工业园区，扩大投资并新建了占地18亩（约12,000.06平方米）、建筑面积约1.5万平方米的湖北爱科斯勒纺织科技有限公司。 这一新厂区专门从事高质量服装衬布的研发和生产。 2018年，为应对市场需求增长和提升产品质量，公司在原2348军工厂区租用60000平方米的生产车间及办公楼，成立湖北省蒲纤纺织科技有限公司。 新厂区配备有，加弹机（8台），整经机4条线，自动穿扣机一条线，织布机364台、高温染缸机8台、拉幅定型机5台及5条双点涂层生产线，一条拉幅双点涂层线，完成一条龙的生产型企业，大大增强了公司的生产能力和产品质量。

After a decade of solid growth, the company expanded its investment in the hometown of the filial son Dong Yong in Hubei — Xiaogan City, Miaotou Industrial Park, constructing the new Hubei Excellent Textile Technology Co., Ltd., covering 18 mu (approximately 12,000.06 square meters) with a building area of about 15,000 square meters. This new factory specializes in the research and development and production of high-quality garment interlinings. In 2018, to meet increasing market demand and enhance product quality, the company leased a 60,000 square meter production workshop and office building in the former 2348 military industrial zone, establishing Hubei Puqian Textile Technology Co., Ltd. The new factory is equipped with 8 texturing machines, 4 Warping machine, 1 automatic threading machine, 364 looms, 8 high-temperature dyeing vats, 5 stenter machines, and 5 double-dot coating production lines, along with 1 stenter double-dot coating line, forming an integrated production enterprise that significantly enhances the company's production capacity and product quality.
产品与技术
Products and Technology

公司产品线涵盖彩色衬布、隐形衬布、弹力衬布、经编衬布、针织衬布、男女西服衬布、四面弹衬布、特殊衬布、梭织衬布、树脂衬布、裤腰衬布、双面胶和领衬等多种类型。 我们的现代化生产设备和先进的检测技术，有效解决了高科技含量面料的粘合与转移难题，充分满足现代服装对“轻、薄、软、挺、弹、飘、透”等多方面的需求。 致力于成为服装内衬材料领域的领导者，不断追求技术创新和产品卓越，以满足和超越客户的期望。

The company's product line includes colored interlinings, invisible interlinings, stretch interlinings, warp-knitted interlinings, knitted interlinings, men's and women's suit interlinings, four-way stretch interlinings, special interlinings, woven interlinings, resin interlinings, waistband interlinings, double-sided adhesives, and collar interlinings, among others. Our modern production equipment and advanced testing technology effectively solve the bonding and transfer problems of high-tech content fabrics, fully meeting the diverse needs of modern clothing for "light, thin, soft, firm, elastic, drapey, and breathable" characteristics. Committed to becoming a leader in the garment interlining materials field, continuously pursuing technological innovation and product excellence to meet and exceed customer expectations.
